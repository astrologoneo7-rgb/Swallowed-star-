Swallowed Star

Is a Chinese sci-fi anime set in a future where humanity fights powerful monsters after a global disaster. The story follows Luo Feng, a young fighter who trains to become stronger and protect humanity while uncovering advanced technology, space civilizations, and cosmic threats. It combines action, martial arts, futuristic technology, and space adventure.
